How to Cook Stir Fry

  1. Basic Ingredients:
    • Brown Rice
    • Carrots
    • Brussel Sprouts
    • Potatoes
    • Tomato
    • Salmon
  2. Basic Spices
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Ginger
    • Onion Powder
    • Garlic Powder
  3. Basic Supplies
    • Frying Pan
    • Olive Oil
    • Spatula

Learn how to prepare vegetables in different ways

1. Prepare the Veggies and Rice

Wash the vegetables and combine one cup of rice with two cups of water in your rice cooker.

2. Cut and Fry all the Veggies

Cut the veggies in bite sized pieces. Oil the pan and cook all the veggies until soft.

3. Combine Everything

Combine the rice with the cooked vegtables and add the spices.
